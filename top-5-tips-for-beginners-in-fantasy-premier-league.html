Top 5 Tips for Beginners in Fantasy Premier League

New to Fantasy Premier League (FPL)? Getting started can be overwhelming, but with these five tips, you'll be on your way to a successful season:

  • Pick a Balanced Squad When building your team, it’s important to spread your budget across all positions rather than spending too much on a few star players. A balanced squad will give you consistent points across the board. Invest in a mix of high-performing and budget-friendly players to ensure you have a strong team throughout the season.
  • Monitor Player Form Look beyond the big names and focus on players who are currently in form. Players who have been performing well in recent matches are more likely to continue their good form. Regularly check player stats and recent performances to make informed decisions.
  • Use Your Wildcard Wisely The Wildcard is a powerful tool that allows you to make unlimited transfers in a single gameweek. Save it for when you really need to overhaul your team, such as after a transfer window or during a period of fixture congestion. Using it strategically can give you a significant advantage.
  • Keep an Eye on Injuries Injuries can derail your fantasy team if you're not careful. Make it a habit to check injury updates before making your final squad decisions. Ensure your team is filled with players who are fit and likely to start, and have substitutes ready for any unexpected injuries.
  • Don’t Rush Transfers Transfers can be tempting, especially if a player has a standout performance. However, it’s wise to wait until closer to the gameweek deadline to make transfers. This allows you to avoid making changes based on temporary form and ensures you’re not caught out by midweek injuries or player rotations.
