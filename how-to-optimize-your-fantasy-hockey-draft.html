How to Optimize Your Fantasy Hockey Draft

A well-executed draft is key to a successful Fantasy Hockey season. Here’s how to optimize your draft strategy:

  • Prioritize Forwards Forwards typically score more points than defensemen. Aim to secure top forwards early in the draft to build a strong offensive lineup. Their goal-scoring potential and contributions to assists make them high-value picks.
  • Keep an Eye on Defensemen Defensemen who contribute offensively can be valuable assets. Look for defensemen who score goals and add assists, as they can provide additional points beyond defensive stats.
  • Research Goalies Goalies can be game-changers in Fantasy Hockey. Draft reliable goalies who play for teams with strong defensive setups. Goalies who frequently start and have good save percentages can provide a significant points boost.
  • Stay Informed on Pre-Season Changes Pay attention to off-season moves and training camp performances. Player trades, line changes, and new team dynamics can impact player performance. Staying informed will help you make better draft decisions.
